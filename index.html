FAQ

Frequently asked questions

About Meta Ads for coaches, our process, and how we work.

What ROAS can I realistically expect from Meta Ads?
Most of our coaching and EdTech clients see between 4x and 12x ROAS within the first 90 days. The exact number depends on your offer price, audience, and funnel quality — which is exactly what we build and optimise for you.
How much ad budget do I need to get started?
We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹30,000 per month to see meaningful results. Most clients start at ₹50,000–75,000 per month and scale from there as ROAS improves.
How long before I see results from Meta Ads?
Most clients start seeing qualified leads within the first 7–14 days. Significant revenue impact typically shows within 30–60 days. Our fastest result was ₹8L in 30 days for Basically Felicia.
Do you work with coaches who have never run ads before?
Yes — as long as you have a proven offer that has sold before (even organically). We'll build your entire paid system from scratch. What we don't do is help create an unproven offer.
What makes House of ROAS different from other Meta Ads agencies?
Most agencies run ads. We build the full system: Meta Ads + sales funnel + WhatsApp automation + live tracking — all working together. We're also exclusively focused on coaches and EdTech, which means we know your audience better than any generalist agency.
Do I need to be on Instagram to work with you?
No. Meta Ads run on Facebook and Instagram. We can target your ideal audience on either or both platforms regardless of your own follower count. Many of our clients had under 10,000 followers when they started.
What does your fee structure look like?
Book a free strategy call and we'll share a customised proposal based on your goals and ad budget. No hidden fees, no lock-in contracts.
Can you guarantee results?
We don't guarantee specific numbers — no honest agency can. What we do guarantee is that you'll get our full system, our best strategy, and daily optimisation. Our track record speaks for itself: 80% of clients triple their revenue within 6 months.
